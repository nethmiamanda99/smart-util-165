IoT-based Mobile Applications for Sustainable Resource Management

Dr. V. Suma's article [1] explores IoT's role in diverse agriculture, focusing on predictive analysis, cloud-managed IoT devices, and security. Smart agriculture's potential to enhance food production, resource management, and labor productivity is highlighted, using past knowledge and IoT tools. The integration of IoT in agriculture has enabled monitoring and control, transforming traditional farming through cloud-based data collection [1].
IoT's significance lies in cost-effective operations, scalable software integration, hardware deployment, and intelligent decision-making for smart farming. The article underscores the importance of integrating advanced IoT devices to optimize resource utilization and productivity in agriculture [1].Challenges of merging modern tech into conventional farming are addressed, emphasizing proper training for successful IoT adoption [1].

Energy-Efficient Mobile Agent Protocol for Secure IoT Sustainable Applications

In their paper "Energy-Efficient Mobile Agent Protocol for Secure IoT Sustainable Applications" [2], Elhoseny et al. introduce a mobile agent-based protocol for secure and energy-efficient IoT applications. This protocol employs mobile agents to intelligently collect data from sensor nodes, selecting optimal cluster heads based on node parameters. Secure communication is ensured through trust, authentication, and encryption mechanisms. Simulation results demonstrate its superiority over existing alternatives in terms of energy efficiency, latency, and data transmission. The protocol offers a reliable solution for secure IoT communication, with potential for future improvements using machine learning to enhance performance.
